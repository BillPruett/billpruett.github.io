Biography

Chris Bennett

After I first stroked the strings of a guitar from my dad when I was 14, I could never put it down. Music is not just a passion for me, it is life.

My love of music sprang out like it did for many other teenagers in the 90s, and rock music stroked my soul. I have soaked in much of the Raleigh night scene, performing the sounds of rock and blues through original music and old time favorites.

My musical style expanded when I attended the NC School of the Arts and East Carolina University to study classical guitar. Another great experience was learning more about jazz and receiving a Bachelor of Music degree from NC Central University, where I was privileged to learn from some of the most talented musicians in the world.

To me, there is no limit to the genres of music, be it classical, rock, jazz, blues, gypsy, flamenco, I totally immerse myself into the sound of music and indulge the joy, energy, comfort, and relaxation it brings to me and my audience. I invite you to join me on the journey of music and explore the possibilities!

    Education:

  • East Carolina University, Master of Music in Guitar Performance
  • North Carolina Central University, Bachelor of Music
